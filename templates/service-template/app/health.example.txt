Example health endpoint requirement:

GET /health

Expected response:

HTTP 200 OK

{
"status": "healthy",
"service": "example-service"
}

Every service onboarded to the platform should expose a health endpoint so that CI/CD, Kubernetes, ECS, load balancers and monitoring tools can validate service availability.

Health checks help the platform confirm whether the service is running, reachable and safe to receive traffic.
